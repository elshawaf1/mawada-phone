Welcome to Mawada Phone ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

  • Theme preference (Light/Dark mode)

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect browsing history outside the App
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Sentry, or similar)
  • We do not collect data from social media accounts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your account, authenticating your identity, and providing password recovery.
  • Order Processing: Processing purchases, managing order history, tracking order status, and coordinating deliveries.
  • Payment Processing: Facilitating secure payments through our payment processor (Paymob). We do not store your credit card details.
  • Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries and resolving issues.
  • Push Notifications: Sending order status updates, delivery notifications, and promotional offers (you can opt out at any time through your device settings).
  • App Improvement: Maintaining and improving the App's functionality and user experience.

4. Payment Information

We use Paymob as our third-party payment processor. When you make a payment:

  • Payment card details (card number, CVV, expiry date) are entered directly into the Paymob native payment interface and never touch our application or servers.
  • We only receive a payment confirmation or failure status from Paymob.
  • Paymob is PCI-DSS compliant and handles all sensitive payment data securely.

Supported payment methods include:

  • Visa / Mastercard (via Paymob card integration)
  • Digital Wallets: Vodafone Cash, Orange Cash, We Pay (via Paymob wallet integration)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Supabase Cloud: Your account data, orders, addresses, and preferences are stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Local Device Storage: Session tokens, language preferences, theme settings, and recent searches are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

5.2 Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • All data transmission between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS.
  • Payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant Paymob.
  • Session tokens are automatically refreshed to maintain secure access.

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Order data: Retained for a minimum of 5 years for legal and accounting purposes.
  • Push notification tokens: Deleted when you log out of the App.
  • Local data: Stored on your device until you clear it or uninstall the App.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processing: Necessary transaction data is shared with Paymob to process your payment.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred with appropriate notice.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all personal data we hold about you through the App's profile section.
  • Edit: Update or correct your personal information (name, phone, email, addresses) directly in the App.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at elshawafa04@gmail.com.
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your personal data in a commonly used format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the App at any time. Account deletion will remove your data from our servers.

If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.
